Strategy lab

The Strategy lab is the honest backtest workflow: tune on in-sample dates, freeze the config, then score on out-of-sample dates you did not optimize against.

Three run modes

ButtonWhat it doesTrust level
Exploratory Day Single-day backtest for quick dev feedback ⚠️ Not evidence — labeled exploratory
Commit In-Sample Freezes strategy JSON against your in-sample date window Training set only
Evaluate Out-of-Sample Runs the frozen commit on a new date range ✅ Headline honest metrics

Recommended workflow

  1. Exploratory Day — sanity-check one ticker/date. Confirm trades look plausible before burning time on a full window.
  2. Set in-sample dates — pick a range with enough trading days in your SQLite cache. Wider is not always better; avoid tuning on the same days you will evaluate.
  3. Commit In-Sample — saves a commitId. Strategy parameters are frozen; you cannot accidentally change them mid-evaluation.
  4. Set out-of-sample dates — must not overlap in-sample. Click Evaluate Out-of-Sample.
  5. Read the verdict — max drawdown headlines, net after costs, and an overfitting warning if in-sample looked unrealistically good.
Exploratory results are yellow for a reason. They appear in the UI with an “not evidence” banner. Do not share exploratory P&L as proof of edge.

Strategy parameters

  • Entry — daily high breakout at bar open (running high, no future data).
  • Position size — USD notional per trade.
  • Stop / take-profit — weighted TP legs plus hard stop.
  • Auto exit time — force flat before close (default 14:00 market time).
  • Risk caps — max daily loss and max concurrent trades.

Preview DSL exports the parsed strategy text for auditing. See Honesty by design for cost defaults and metrics definitions.
